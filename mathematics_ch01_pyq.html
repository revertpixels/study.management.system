Topic Analysis

Time and Work questions distribution in recent CDS examinations:

  • Basic Time and Work 35%
  • Pipes and Cisterns 25%
  • Work and Wages 20%
  • Efficiency Based Problems 15%
  • Other Sub-topics 5%

Examination Strategy

Based on analysis of previous year questions, here are some strategies for Time and Work problems:

  • The average number of Time and Work questions in CDS exam is 3-4 out of 100 questions.
  • Questions typically involve basic concepts but may require multiple steps to solve.
  • Practice calculating LCM quickly as it's essential for solving multiple workers problems.
  • Focus on Pipes and Cisterns as they appear more frequently in recent papers.
  • Keep track of units (days, hours, minutes) carefully throughout your calculations.
